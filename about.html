Approximately 82.4 million people worldwide were forcibly displaced in 2020 with 4.1 million asylum seekers.

Refugee children are five times as likely to be out of school than their non-refugee peers.

Factors such as lack of transportation and identification, financial issues, and the presence of language and cultural barriers prevent refugee children from readily accessing education.

Only 3% of refugees enroll in college or university compared to the world average of 37%.
